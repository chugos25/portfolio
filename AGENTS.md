# AGENTS.md

## Project purpose

This repository contains the professional portfolio website of Hugo Sánchez Nieto.

The website is recruiter-facing and designed for junior opportunities in:
- business analysis,
- marketing analytics,
- CRM / BI,
- consumer insights,
- performance marketing,
- digital strategy,
- growth,
- agencies,
- Big 4,
- consulting,
- multinational companies.

Automotive is a sector of interest and appears in some projects, but the website must not position Hugo as an automotive-only candidate.

## Tone

Use a professional, rigorous, concise tone.

Avoid inflated language:
- expert
- guru
- visionary
- passionate
- rockstar
- world-class

Prefer evidence-based language:
- analysed
- structured
- modelled
- translated into recommendations
- projected
- contributed
- learned and applied

## Positioning

Automotive specialist
Data scientist
Product manager

Senior consultant

## Privacy

## Academic and group work integrity

Always label:
- group projects as group projects
- projected metrics as projected
- confidential professional data as anonymized or synthetic
- courses in progress as in progress

Do not claim certificates as completed unless explicitly provided.

## IGI Global project

This project must emphasize:
- self-directed learning
- taking a thesis beyond the minimum academic requirement
- learning SEM/CFA/MANOVA independently
- turning a 10/10 Honors thesis into a peer-reviewed academic chapter
- translating consumer research into business implications

Do not overclaim expertise.
Use “learned and applied”, not “mastered”.
